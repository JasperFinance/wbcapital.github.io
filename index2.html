What we do

i

Market Research

Research notes on real estate and financial markets, tracking pricing, yields, capital flows and the macro drivers shaping them.

  • Sector and submarket reviews
  • Rents, yields, take-up, vacancy, pipeline
  • Public sources: Land Registry, ONS, REIT reporting
ii

Due Diligence

Screening investments against return hurdles and risk factors to support clear buy, hold or exit reasoning.

  • Financials and income security
  • Lease structure, covenant strength, WAULT
  • ESG factors including EPC and BREEAM
iii

Modelling

Valuation, DCF and development appraisal models, stress-tested to show where a deal holds and where it breaks.

  • Cash flow and appraisal builds
  • Sensitivity on yield, growth and cost
  • Viability and return analysis

How we work

Public sources only

Every figure traces back to something a reader can verify: Land Registry, ONS, company reporting, regulator and agency data. No black boxes.

Assumptions you can defend

Models are only as good as the inputs behind them. We state assumptions plainly and stress-test them, so the conclusion holds up to a hard question.

Reasoning, not noise

Output is built to support a decision. Clear argument, the numbers behind it, and an honest read on what could go wrong.

About WB Capital

WB Capital was founded to do one thing well: produce rigorous, source-led research across real estate and finance. The work draws on training in property valuation, real estate financial modelling and investment analysis, applied to live UK markets.

We are deliberately narrow. We research and analyse. We do not manage money, raise capital, or carry out regulated investment activity. That focus is what lets the work stay independent and the reasoning stay honest.

The aim is research that genuinely sharpens a decision, not analysis for its own sake.
